Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11/22/2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our diagram-generating GPT service and the associated SVG to PNG conversion API ("the Service").

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal or sensitive information from users. The Service processes the following types of data:

  • Input Data: Textual inputs (e.g., instructions to generate diagrams) and SVG code submitted via the API. This data is used solely for generating and converting diagrams.
  • Automatically Collected Data: Logs (e.g., request URLs, time of access, and errors) for debugging purposes. These logs do not include personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information provided to:

  • Generate SVG diagrams and convert them to PNG images.
  • Improve the functionality and performance of the Service.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Input data is processed in memory and discarded after the response is sent. Logs may be retained for up to one year for debugging purposes.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to third parties, except as required by law or to comply with legal obligations.
